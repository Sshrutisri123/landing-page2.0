Cloud Computing

Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services over the internet, allowing users to access and store data and applications on remote servers rather than on local hardware. This technology offers flexibility, scalability, and cost efficiency, enabling businesses and individuals to manage resources dynamically. Cloud computing supports a wide range of services, including data storage, networking, and software applications, fostering innovation and collaboration across various sectors.
